5th Annual Reel Paddling Film Festival & Silent Auction

Event Date and Time: 
Fri, 03/29/2013 - 6:00pm - 10:00pm
Location: 
Abromson Center, 88 Bedford Street, USM Portland Campus


The Film Fest: A great collection of films served to you at the ideal time of year. Late March we're aching for the sea and so what better way to spend a Friday evening than watching paddlers amidst beautifully filmed landscapes?! Hope you'll join us.

The Silent Auction: A great collection of auction items ranging from Kokotat PFDs, Lendal paddles, Weekend Getaways, local Maine art and MUCH MORE! Come early, bid early (and often). Our silent auction is one of MITA's largest fundraisers and to make the experience all the better a cash bar and light refreshments are available!

Tickets: $12 advance, $15 door, $10 studentTickets will be available from the MITA website: www.mita.org

Contact Name: 
Maine Island Trail Association
Contact Phone: 
207-761-8225
Contact Email: 
info@mita.org
